About Us

Incorporation:

An-Najah Academy is an accredited and licensed training institution in the Sultanate of Oman from the Ministry of Education higher education, scientific research and innovation with commercial register no 2208369, headquartered in Dhofar Governorate, Salalah State, and the Academy was established in 2005

  • Vision:

    • Continuous excellence in human development to achieve positive self-change

  • About the Founder
  • He co-founded Dhofar University and was the coordinator for the integration of the National College of Science and Technology into the Dhofar University project.
  • Assistant to the President of Dhofar University for Student Affairs from 2004 to 2007
  • Vice Dean and Lecturer at the National College of Science and Technology from 1998 to 2004
  • Former CEO of the National Company for Education and Training Services from 1996 to 1998
  • Previously Professor of Psychological Counseling and Educational Sciences at the Department of Education at Dhofar University
  • Founder of Dr. Ahmed bin Ali Al-Mashani Center for Psychological and Family Counseling
  • Founder of enlightenment therapy and registered with the Ministry of Commerce in the Sultanate since 2017.

  • Received the award of His Highness the Ruler of Ajman in 2010 on the occasion of Flag Day
  • Recipient of the Arab League Award for his contributions to the service of family, women and children affairs 2021
  • Member of the American Psychological Association APA since 2019
  • He won first place in the short story writing competition from the Ministry of Education and Youth in 1987

  • He worked as an employee in the MOE from 1979 to 1989
  • A member and representative of the Sultanate in the Arab Federation for Exhibitions and Conferences of the League of Arab States

  • Member of the Writers and Writers Association in the Sultanate

Why An-Najah Academy for Human Development

The Academy works on several goals: - Qualifying Omani job seekers and providing them with results-based thinking strategies, innovative thinking and problem-solving. -Training and qualifying young entrepreneurs to practice self-employment activities and self-reliance. - Enlightening job seekers with the natural resources of the Sultanate and training them to set up their individual projects to invest in them. - Developing the competence of people and institutions by analyzing their training needs and providing them with the skills and knowledge that enhance the quality of their performance in their professions and businesses. -Spreading the culture of human development in society among individuals and institutions. - Conducting research and studies related to human development in the Sultanate. -Enhancing innovative thinking and problem-solving skills for individuals and institutions. - Holding conferences and seminars, and holding exhibitions and festivals in support of human development.
